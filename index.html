I had a chance to meet people from different countries and learn more about American culture. I have always been passionate about every aspect of technology and I take pleasure in learning new things each and every day in the technology field.

With a Solid understanding of web technology and the Ability to design, build and deploy a web application using latest technologies on the server. Devoted IT professional, I constantly challenge myself in staying up-to-date in the technology field.
